About

Hi there, my name is Kenneth Parit Likimani. Welcome to my internet brain. The purpose of this website is to make available for FREE, Kenyan Education for primary and secondary schools level. This comes as a result of three main factors that I consider very important for the future of Kenya.

First and foremost, when students finish primary school eductaion, there are very few secondary schools that can cater for the total number of qualifying candidates. There has seen a steady increase over the years of the number of students missing formal secondary education.

Secondly and of vital importance is the numerous number of strikes held by teachers claiming an increase in pay. These strikes are maliscious because the teachers target exam periods to conduct their strikes. The end result is students getting confused during critical mements of their lives.At the same time these students a learning to be professional strikers - a disaster of the future that we will avert.

Third, is to safeguard the future of Kenya. This will be accomplished by ensuring stundents get consistent learning experience that relates to reality. A typical example is the current education system teaching only english and swahili but when the student joins the real world s/he has to apply sheng to achieve results. This causes mental challenges and frustrations hence lack of focus. There are other numerous examples that will be addressed during these courses.

In conclusion, we are open to ideas, volunteers, mother-tongue translators and proposals to improve the learning environment and achieve our countrys development goals.We would like to also educate these striking teachers: "MONEY WILL NOT MAKE YOU HAPPY". Therefore, we removed money from the picture. This knowledge is FREE to access, and download. To all Kenyan student, enjoy learning. Thank you.
